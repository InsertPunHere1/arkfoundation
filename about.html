The ARK Foundation is an organization dedicated to scientific innovation and human progress. As the world's leading scientific power, we have provided countless contributions toward technological advancement.

"The new Einstein"

Alexander Fox believed that our civilization was at a crisis point, and the only solution would be through Science and technology. in 2003, at the young age of 24, he created the ARK Foundation in order to fulfill his vision of scientific advancement and set out to change the world. Fox quickly found success with the Aether Battery, which harnessed the power of nuclear energy, in a safe, and portable device. This lead to the creation of the very first ARK Phone. We cannot begin to list the number of groundbreaking inventions that Fox personally designed, but there aren’t many humans on the planet that aren’t using some type of ARK device. Tragically, just four years after creating the company that would change the world, Alexander Fox left his laboratory one ordinary night and never returned.
